🟢 Phase 1 도입부 패턴 5개

질문을 듣고 당황하지 않고, 자연스럽게 주제를 던지며 시간을 버는 마법의 문장들

1 When it comes to [주제], I'd have to say my favorite is [구체적 대상].
[주제]에 대해 말하자면, 제가 가장 좋아하는 건 [구체적 대상]이에요.
음악

When it comes to music, I'd have to say my favorite is K-pop.

음식점

When it comes to restaurants, I'd have to say my favorite is the one near my office.

국내여행

When it comes to domestic travel, I'd have to say my favorite is Jeju Island.

2 I'm going to tell you about [대상]. It's a place/thing I really love.
[대상]에 대해 말해볼게요. 제가 정말 좋아하는 곳/것이거든요.
헬스클럽

I'm going to tell you about my gym. It's a place I really love.

집

I'm going to tell you about my house. It's a place I really love.

영화

I'm going to tell you about movies. It's a thing I really love.

3 Actually, there are many [주제] in my country, but I usually go to [대상].
사실 우리나라엔 많은 [주제]가 있지만, 전 주로 [대상]에 가요.
해외여행

Actually, there are many travel options, but I usually go to Japan.

공연

Actually, there are many concerts in my country, but I usually go to K-pop shows.

쇼핑

Actually, there are many shopping malls in my country, but I usually go to Gangnam.

4 Let me tell you about my favorite [대상]. It is located in [장소].
제가 가장 좋아하는 [대상]을 말해볼게요. 거긴 [장소]에 위치해 있어요.
음식점

Let me tell you about my favorite restaurant. It is located in Hongdae.

헬스클럽

Let me tell you about my favorite gym. It is located near my apartment.

직장

Let me tell you about my workplace. It is located in Seoul.

5 I frequently visit/use [대상] whenever I have free time.
전 시간 날 때마다 [대상]을 자주 방문해요/사용해요.
자유시간

I frequently visit cafes whenever I have free time.

인터넷

I frequently use the internet whenever I have free time.

텔레비전

I frequently watch TV whenever I have free time.

🔵 Phase 2 전개 패턴 10개

본격적으로 외관, 시설, 분위기, 그리고 거기서 내가 주로 하는 행동을 묘사하는 템플릿

1The first thing you notice is that it is very [형용사].
딱 처음 보면 거기가 아주 [넓은/아늑한/현대적인 등]하다는 걸 알 수 있어요.
집

The first thing you notice is that it is very cozy.

헬스클럽

The first thing you notice is that it is very spacious.

호텔

The first thing you notice is that it is very modern.

2It has a great atmosphere, so many people visit there to [동사].
분위기가 엄청 좋아서, 많은 사람들이 [~하러] 거길 찾아요.
음식점

It has a great atmosphere, so many people visit there to enjoy Korean food.

공연

It has a great atmosphere, so many people visit there to enjoy live music.

지형

It has a great atmosphere, so many people visit there to take pictures.

3What I like most about this [대상] is [명사/동명사].
이 [대상]에서 제가 가장 좋아하는 점은 [~라는 것]이에요.
집

What I like most about this house is the view from the window.

직장

What I like most about this company is the flexible work hours.

가전제품

What I like most about this appliance is its convenience.

4Inside the [장소], you can see [명사] everywhere.
[장소] 안에 들어가면, 어디서나 [~]을 볼 수 있어요.
쇼핑

Inside the shopping mall, you can see various brands everywhere.

호텔

Inside the hotel, you can see beautiful decorations everywhere.

헬스클럽

Inside the gym, you can see exercise equipment everywhere.

5They offer a wide variety of [명사].
거긴 아주 다양한 [메뉴/서비스/시설 등]을 제공해요.
음식점

They offer a wide variety of Korean dishes.

헬스클럽

They offer a wide variety of fitness programs.

호텔

They offer a wide variety of room types.

6As for the exterior/interior, it looks very [형용사].
외관/내부에 대해 말하자면, 굉장히 [깔끔해/전통적이어] 보여요.
집

As for the interior, it looks very clean and organized.

미용실

As for the interior, it looks very stylish.

은행

As for the exterior, it looks very modern.

7It takes about [숫자] minutes to get there from my house.
우리 집에서 거기까지 가는 데 한 [숫자]분 정도 걸려요.
직장

It takes about 30 minutes to get there from my house.

헬스클럽

It takes about 10 minutes to get there from my house.

병원

It takes about 15 minutes to get there from my house.

8I usually go there with [사람].
전 보통 거길 [친구들/가족]이랑 같이 가요.
영화

I usually go there with my friends.

가족/친구

I usually go there with my family.

모임

I usually go there with my coworkers.

9Whenever I am there, I normally [동사].
거기 갈 때마다, 전 보통 [~해요].
자유시간

Whenever I am there, I normally read a book and drink coffee.

헬스클럽

Whenever I am there, I normally do weight training for an hour.

국내여행

Whenever I am there, I normally try the local food.

10It is fully equipped with [명사].
거긴 [~]이 아주 잘 갖춰져 있어요.
헬스클럽

It is fully equipped with modern exercise machines.

집

It is fully equipped with the latest home appliances.

호텔

It is fully equipped with comfortable furniture and free Wi-Fi.

🟠 Phase 3 감정 패턴 10개

OPIc 채점관이 AL/IH를 줄 때 가장 중요하게 보는 '나의 감정과 이유'를 덧붙이는 치트키 문장들

1I really love this [대상] because it helps me relieve my stress.
제가 이 [대상]을 정말 좋아하는 이유는 제 스트레스를 풀어주기 때문이에요.
음악

I really love music because it helps me relieve my stress.

자전거

I really love cycling because it helps me relieve my stress.

영화

I really love watching movies because it helps me relieve my stress.

2The staff/people there are always kind and welcoming.
거기 직원들/사람들은 항상 친절하고 반갑게 맞아줘요.
미용실

The staff at the hair salon are always kind and welcoming.

병원

The staff at the hospital are always kind and welcoming.

음식점

The people at the restaurant are always kind and welcoming.

3It makes me feel [감정] whenever I use/visit it.
그걸 사용할/방문할 때마다 전 [편안한/활기찬] 기분이 들어요.
건강

It makes me feel energetic whenever I work out.

음악

It makes me feel relaxed whenever I listen to music.

국내여행

It makes me feel refreshed whenever I travel.

4To be honest, I can't imagine my life without [대상].
솔직히 말해서, [대상] 없는 제 삶은 상상할 수가 없네요.
인터넷

To be honest, I can't imagine my life without the internet.

전화기

To be honest, I can't imagine my life without my smartphone.

음악

To be honest, I can't imagine my life without music.

5It's totally worth the time and money.
시간과 돈을 투자할 가치가 충분해요.
헬스클럽

The gym membership is totally worth the time and money.

해외여행

Traveling abroad is totally worth the time and money.

공연

Going to concerts is totally worth the time and money.

6For these reasons, it has become a huge part of my daily routine.
이런 이유들 때문에, 그건 제 일상생활의 아주 큰 부분이 되었어요.
건강

For these reasons, working out has become a huge part of my daily routine.

음식

For these reasons, cooking has become a huge part of my daily routine.

인터넷

For these reasons, browsing the internet has become a huge part of my daily routine.

7I feel so refreshed after I finish [동명사].
[~을] 끝내고 나면 기분이 정말 상쾌해져요.
건강

I feel so refreshed after I finish exercising.

쇼핑

I feel so refreshed after I finish shopping.

자전거

I feel so refreshed after I finish cycling.

8It's the perfect place/way to take a break from my busy life.
제 바쁜 일상 속에서 휴식을 취하기에 완벽한 곳/방법이에요.
국내여행

Traveling is the perfect way to take a break from my busy life.

자유시간

Going to a cafe is the perfect way to take a break from my busy life.

집에서 보내는 휴가

Staying home is the perfect way to take a break from my busy life.

9I was really impressed by its [명사].
전 그것의 [퀄리티/서비스]에 정말 깊은 인상을 받았어요.
음식점

I was really impressed by its food quality.

호텔

I was really impressed by its customer service.

가전제품

I was really impressed by its performance.

10It totally fits my personal taste.
그건 완전 제 개인적인 취향에 딱 맞아요.
패션

This brand's style totally fits my personal taste.

음악

This genre of music totally fits my personal taste.

가구

This kind of furniture totally fits my personal taste.

🔴 Phase 4 마무리 패턴 5개

말문이 막혀서 정적이 흐르기 전에, '내 답변은 여기까지야'라고 깔끔하게 치고 빠지는 문장들

1So, this is everything I can say about [대상].
그래서, 이게 제가 [대상]에 대해 말할 수 있는 전부예요.
집

So, this is everything I can say about my house.

음식점

So, this is everything I can say about my favorite restaurant.

직장

So, this is everything I can say about my workplace.

2Overall, I highly recommend this [대상] to everyone.
전반적으로, 전 이 [대상]을 모두에게 강력히 추천해요.
음식점

Overall, I highly recommend this restaurant to everyone.

헬스클럽

Overall, I highly recommend this gym to everyone.

가전제품

Overall, I highly recommend this product to everyone.

3If you have a chance, you should definitely check it out.
기회가 된다면, 꼭 한 번 가보세요/써보세요.
국내여행

If you have a chance, you should definitely visit Busan.

공연

If you have a chance, you should definitely see a K-pop concert.

음식

If you have a chance, you should definitely try Korean BBQ.

4To sum up, [대상] is my absolute favorite.
요약하자면, [대상]은 제 최애예요.
음악

To sum up, K-pop is my absolute favorite.

음식

To sum up, Korean BBQ is my absolute favorite.

영화

To sum up, action movies are my absolute favorite.

5I'm looking forward to going/using there again soon.
조만간 또 거기 가길/그걸 쓰길 기대하고 있어요.
해외여행

I'm looking forward to traveling abroad again soon.

공연

I'm looking forward to going to a concert again soon.

헬스클럽

I'm looking forward to going to the gym again soon.
